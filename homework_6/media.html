Hunting Under the Umber

I was hunting out under the umber
The wind in the trees filled my ears with their sighs
The dominion of humankind quiet in slumber
The moon on my back and the stars in my eyes

I move through this life in the throes of reflection
my body a visage that i cannot see
to be lost from my home, it was not my election
their minds they are distant and muted to me

I've a lover who's strong as a banyan
I've a lover with mind old and wise
I've a lover who drinks deep from life with abandon
And she looks deep with love at the cat in my eyes

    

Deep in the shadows, i found her in my Bhava
   Her breath it came even, her form in repose
She opened her eyes, touched me with her Alaya
  Her paws crossed beneath her, warm cat in the snows

 

Her mind swept through me, a wind of perception
a low gentle purring, that echoed my own
as my dichotomistic mind silenced it’s question
the truth became certain, creation my home

Now we’ll be walking out under my umber
The wind in our trees fill our ears with their sighs
The minds of our people caress like a lover
The stars on our backs and our moons in our eyes
